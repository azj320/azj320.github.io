My Bakery

Nov, 2022

Overview

Developed a bakery app in React with AntDesign. Users can sort items by popularity, calories, and prices. Users can also add/remove items to see the total calories through checking/uncheck the checkbox on each card. For users who have special request, the app can only display items that are gluten-free, dairy-free or both.
